- About Me -

This is Ian Hsu. As a Product Manager, Digital Marketer, and traveler. I love inventing, breaking the rules, making mistakes, taking the risk, experimenting, and have fun. I would like to start an idea with hands-on prototyping to high-level programming.

I can work alone efficiently, but collaborating with people from diverse backgrounds is much more fun. I believe live and work can have a perfect balance and have a significant life with no regret. Building upon my solid academic background in engineering and extensive work experience in information management in the media industry, I am seeking to enhance my abilities of user experience (UX) design, coding skills and develop new technology with considerations of technical feasibility, user desirability, and business viability.

My advice to new grads, after two years in the wild

After getting my computer science degree from Duke in 2015, I joined Google as a software engineer and co-founded Dancing Pineapple. These first two years in tech and music were filled with a rapid succession of ups and downs, and plenty of hard-won lessons.

I know that these lessons would have been really useful for me after my own graduation, so I decided to take some time to share some actionable advice for those of you just starting out in the real world.
